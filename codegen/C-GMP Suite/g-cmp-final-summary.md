## COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Do I HAVE to follow all 6 phases?**
A: YES. Non-negotiable. If you skip any, you risk mistakes.

**Q: What if my fix is "simple"?**
A: Doesn't matter. All fixes go through 6 phases. Simple fixes move fast (30 min), complex fixes move slow (60 min). Both follow same process.

**Q: Can I skip Phase 0 if I'm confident?**
A: NO. Phase 0 catches assumptions you didn't know were wrong. Always run it.

**Q: What if Phase 4 test fails?**
A: STOP. Fix the issue. Re-run ENTIRE Phase 4 (not just the failing test).

**Q: Can I combine phases?**
A: NO. Each phase must complete fully before moving to next.

**Q: When do I output the final report?**
A: After Phase 5 is complete AND all 10 items in Definition of Done are ‚úì.

**Q: Can I add extra features not in the plan?**
A: NO. Document them as follow-up tasks. Complete current task first.

**Q: How long does this take?**
A: 45 minutes average (30 min simple, 60 min complex). Planning is 5 min, coding is 15 min, testing/audit is 25 min.

**Q: Is this really faster than "just coding"?**
A: YES. You avoid rework, mistakes, and debugging. Invest 5 min in Phase -1, save 30 min in rework.
