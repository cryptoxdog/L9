1. **Embodied world models MUST be embedded in R1**, not bolted on
   - Otherwise cognitive efficiency gains disappear
   - Target latency: <100ms for 10-agent coordination

2. **Semantic kernel MUST enforce at OS level**, not app level
   - Otherwise agents circumvent policies
   - Target: Zero policy violations possible

3. **Intention communication MUST achieve 95% reduction**, not 50%
   - Otherwise coordination remains bottleneck
